# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

We are committed to making participation in this project welcoming and
respectful. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, and disruptive or
bad-faith behavior are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior privately to the repository maintainers through
[GitHub](https://github.com/stevencarpenter/token-auditor). Reports will be
reviewed and handled in confidence where possible.
